Ozanam Hollywood Holiday Gala

This will be the 30th year of Ozanam's Hollywood Holiday Gala, which began as a movie benefit in 1983. Throughout the past three decades, it has evolved into Ozanam's largest fundraiser of the year. Recently it was even recognized as one of the "Top 10" Events in Kansas City! Last year, nearly 800 people attended the event and helped us to raise more than $600,000!

Whether you have made this event a holiday tradition or are attending for the first time, we hope you will join us for the 30th Anniversary Hollywood Holiday Gala on Thursday, December 5, 2013, at the Sheraton Kansas City Hotel at Crown Center as we experience the glitz and glamour of Hollywood to benefit Ozanam! This fun-filled evening begins at 5:30 p.m. with a large silent auction featuring more than 300 items. Then guests enjoy an elegant dinner and exciting program with performances by the Ozanam children. Finally, the night is topped off with an oral auction led by the Nigro Brothers Auctioneers, Larry Moore and Bryan Truta. Festivities conclude by 10 p.m. Business attire, holiday clothing, or formal wear is encouraged. Be a star by showing your support!

Our Gala Chairmen this year are Karen & Chuck Peters, who have been longtime supporters of Ozanam. We are also proud to have Rita & Lamar Hunt, Jr. serving as our Honorary Chairmen for the evening. Since the event's inception, Larry Moore has served as our distinguished Master of Ceremonies, and we are pleased to have his participation once again.

All of the proceeds from the Gala support the life-saving programs and services for at-risk youth at Ozanam. Since 1948, Ozanam has provided residential treatment, day treatment, mentoring, transitional living, and school consultation services to hundreds of boys and girls each year. For more information about Ozanam, please visit www.ozanam.org.
