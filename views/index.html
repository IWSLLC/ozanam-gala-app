Ozanam Hollywood Holiday Gala

This will be the 30th year of Ozanam's Hollywood Holiday Gala, which began as a movie benefit in 1983.

 

Last year, nearly 800 people attended the event, helping us to raise about $560,000!

 

All of the proceeds from the Gala fund scholarships for children and families who otherwise would not be able to benefit from the life-saving programs and services that Ozanam provides.
